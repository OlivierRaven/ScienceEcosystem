About ScienceEcosystem

We’re building open, durable infrastructure for the full research lifecycle — search, libraries with PDFs and annotations, and trust features to help you avoid retracted or unreliable work.

What we believe

  • Openness: knowledge is a public good.
  • Collaboration: research advances fastest when we build together.
  • Integrity: provenance and reproducibility are first‑class.
  • Equity: access and recognition should not depend on funding or geography.
  • User‑first: tools should fit naturally into daily work.
  • Sustainability: this is infrastructure for decades.

Data sources & attribution

We rely on open, reputable sources and always show provenance:

  • OpenAlex: works, authors, institutions, venues, concepts.
  • Crossref: DOI metadata, funders, Crossmark status when available.
  • ORCID: researcher identity; sign‑in and public records.
  • Unpaywall: open access locations and OA status.
  • Zotero: library interoperability for collections, export.

Every paper, profile, and venue page includes “View source” chips (e.g., OpenAlex, Crossref, ORCID, OA PDF) so you can verify information and follow it to the original record.
