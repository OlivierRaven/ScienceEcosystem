About ScienceEcosystem

An open home for research that people can trust and use every day.

What is ScienceEcosystem

ScienceEcosystem is a single place to find, save, and understand research. It brings papers, data, code, peer review, and funding into one connected space. It shows where information comes from and links back to the original sources.

Why ScienceEcosystem

The current system slows people down. Paywalls hide knowledge, tools are scattered, and rewards favor journals instead of good work. ScienceEcosystem focuses on clarity, access, and accountability so that scientists can spend more time doing science.

Who it is for

Researchers, students, educators, funders, and anyone curious. If you care about reliable knowledge, ScienceEcosystem is for you.

Principles

  • Open by default. Data and methods should be easy to access and reuse.
  • Integrity. Always show the source and the status of a work, including retractions and versions.
  • Useful first. Every feature should save time and reduce friction.
  • Community owned. ScienceEcosystem is built with and for the people who use it.

Roadmap

  • Now: search, profiles, and a basic library you can trust.
  • Next: open peer review and lightweight collaboration tools.
  • Later: funding tools and reproducible workspaces at scale.

Get involved

  • Researchers: try the search and tell us what is missing.
  • Developers: help build small, reliable pieces that others can reuse.
  • Funders and partners: support work that becomes public infrastructure.
