About ScienceEcosystem

Our Mission

We are creating a unified, open, and intelligent platform that brings together the core elements of modern science: research papers, data, code, peer reviews, funding, and collaboration tools.

Why Now?

Traditional science publishing is outdated. ScienceEcosystem aims to make science faster, fairer, and globally accessible — removing the silos that slow down innovation and trust.

A system where researchers are rewarded not just for publishing, but also for reviewing, sharing reproducible code, mentoring, and collaborating across disciplines.

ScienceEcosystem is not just a platform. It’s a movement to redesign how knowledge is shared, trusted, and used.

Structure

Knowledge Hub

We are creating a unified, open, and intelligent platform that brings together the core elements of modern science: research papers, data, code, peer reviews, funding, and collaboration tools.

Open Peer Review System

A transparent and collaborative review process linked to researcher profiles, giving credit and visibility to reviewers.

Collaborative Research Workspaces

Live project spaces where researchers can co-write, share datasets, track progress, and integrate notebooks and code editors.

Funding & Impact Tools

Tools to connect projects with funders, track grants, and transparently measure research outcomes and societal impact.

Core Integrated Platforms

ORCID

Researcher authentication and profile linking

OpenAlex

arXiv

PubMed

CORE

Crossref

Unpaywall

GitHub

Code sharing, versioning, and open-source integration

Zenodo

Figshare
