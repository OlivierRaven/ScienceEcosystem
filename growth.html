Growth Plan

How We’re Building ScienceEcosystem

ScienceEcosystem is designed to grow in stages — starting with essential tools that address the most urgent barriers in science today, and expanding into a full open research infrastructure.

We are focused on steady, thoughtful development, working closely with early users and institutions to guide each step.

2025: Foundation

  • Launch the public website and early messaging
  • Build the MVP: basic document upload, AI-powered summaries, semantic search
  • Start community outreach across disciplines
  • Begin integration work with ORCID, OpenAlex, and other open infrastructure

2026: Review and Collaboration

  • Introduce open peer review, annotation, and reputation tools
  • Run beta testing with researchers and research groups
  • Begin piloting funding models (microgrants, community prioritization)
  • Build tools to support reproducibility and shared workspaces

2027: Scale and Impact

  • Full platform release with collaboration, publication, and funding systems
  • Build partnerships with institutions, funders, and open science communities
  • Launch citizen science and public education components
  • Support policy translation and global access initiatives
