Why a New Ecosystem?

The current scientific system is slow, fragmented, and driven by outdated incentives. ScienceEcosystem is building a modular platform for transparent publishing, AI-enhanced discovery, open peer review, and smart funding.

Searchable Knowledge Hub

Discover papers, data, and code with powerful AI summaries and filtering.

Open Peer Review

Transparent, reputation-based feedback with AI support and annotation tools.

Collaboration Tools

Live document editing, task planning, and integrated analysis spaces.

Smarter Funding

Microgrants, voting, and transparent budgeting to support innovative work.
