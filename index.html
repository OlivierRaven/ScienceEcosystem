ScienceEcosystem

The unified, open platform for publishing, peer review, collaboration, and funding — all within a transparent and inclusive ecosystem.

What is ScienceEcosystem?

ScienceEcosystem is a next-generation platform built to streamline the entire scientific process. It enables open publishing, transparent peer review, real-time collaboration, and intelligent funding — all in one place. Our mission is to make science more efficient, reproducible, and accessible to all stakeholders: researchers, institutions, funders, and the public.

Challenges in Science Today

Scientific progress is slowed by outdated systems: paywalled journals, slow peer review, siloed data, reproducibility crises, and misaligned incentives. These barriers hinder collaboration, waste resources, and limit the societal impact of research.

Our Vision

We envision a global, open-source scientific community. ScienceEcosystem provides tools for publishing, reviewing, collaborating, and funding — all enhanced with AI. It empowers scientists across disciplines to co-create knowledge and accelerate breakthroughs.

Our Solution

  • Knowledge Hub: Upload and explore interconnected papers, datasets, and code with semantic search and summaries.
  • Open Peer Review: Community-based reviewing with transparency and AI-assisted validation.
  • Collaborative Workspaces: Real-time co-authoring, annotations, data analysis, and project planning tools.
  • Smart Funding: Microgrants, retroactive rewards, and democratic funding mechanisms like quadratic voting.
  • Education & Inclusion: Multilingual support, citizen science modules, and public engagement tools.

Key Features

  • Publishing: Share research papers, datasets, protocols, and code in one integrated platform.
  • AI Tools: Semantic search, auto-summarization, and intelligent literature mapping.
  • Open Peer Review: Transparent, community-driven, reputation-based review process.
  • Collaboration: Shared workspaces, version control, integrated tools (e.g., Jupyter, RStudio).
  • Funding Innovation: Decentralized funding through community input and measurable impact.
  • Accessibility: ORCID integration, multilingual AI translation, and inclusive design.
