ScienceEcosystem

A new platform for publishing, reviewing, collaborating on, and funding science — all in one transparent and AI-enhanced environment.

What is ScienceEcosystem?

ScienceEcosystem is an open platform to unify scientific workflows: from publishing and peer review to collaboration and funding. Built to be transparent, modular, AI-powered, and accessible to everyone — researchers, institutions, funders, and the public. A system where researchers get rewarded not just for publishing but for: Reviewing, Sharing reproducible code, Mentoring and Collaborating across disciplines.

The Problem

Modern science is hindered by systemic inefficiencies: fragmented journals, paywalls, slow peer review, reproducibility issues, and misaligned incentives. This wastes resources, slows progress, and weakens trust.

Our Vision

We imagine science as a global, open-source collaboration. Our platform starts with uploads, search, summaries, and annotation — growing into live collaboration tools, AI-powered review, smart funding, and global access.

The Solution: A Layered Platform for Scientific Progress

  • Knowledge Hub: Upload and discover papers, datasets, and code — all semantically linked and summarized
  • Open Peer Review: Transparent, community-reviewed, with AI-assisted validation
  • Collaborative Workspaces: Real-time co-authoring, annotation, analysis, and planning
  • Smart Funding: Transparent microgrants, retroactive rewards, quadratic voting
  • Education & Inclusion: Tools for citizen science, multilingual support, and public engagement

What Does ScienceEcosystem Do?

It creates a unified ecosystem for the entire scientific process:
  • 🧾 Publishing Upload research papers, datasets, code, protocols — all in one place
  • 🧠 AI Assistance Semantic search, automatic summaries, literature mapping
  • 👥 Open Peer Review Transparent, reputation-based reviewing with community participation
  • 🧪 Collaboration Real-time editing, shared workspaces, version control, integrated tools like Jupyter/R
  • 💸 Smart Funding Models Microgrants, community voting, and funding linked to real impact
  • 🌍 Open Access & Inclusion Citizen science modules, multilingual AI translation, open education
  • 🔗 ORCID Integration Login and author identity via ORCID — no new accounts needed
