ScienceEcosystem

Rebuilding science for the 21st century

What is ScienceEcosystem?

ScienceEcosystem is an open, modular platform where researchers can publish, review, discover, collaborate on, and fund scientific work — all in one transparent and AI-enhanced environment.

The Problem

Modern science is hindered by systemic inefficiencies: fragmented publishing systems, outdated peer review, reproducibility crises, limited access to data and code, and misaligned incentives that prioritise prestige over public good.

Our Vision

We reimagine science as transparent, reproducible, and participatory — more like open-source development than traditional publishing. Starting with uploads, search, summaries, and annotation, we’ll evolve toward deep collaboration, integrated tools, and smarter funding.

The Solution: A Layered Platform for Scientific Progress

  • 1. Knowledge Hub: Upload/search papers, data, code, with AI summaries and version control
  • 2. Open Peer Review: Transparent, reputation-based, AI-assisted feedback
  • 3. Collaborative Workspaces: Live writing, planning, annotation, and integrated analysis tools
  • 4. Smart Funding Models: Microgrants, quadratic voting, budget transparency
  • 5. Inclusion & Education: Citizen science, AI translation, policy tools
