How It Works

ScienceEcosystem provides a modular, integrated environment where researchers can upload work, collaborate in real time, and receive feedback. Core functionalities include:

  • Upload & tag publications, data, and code
  • Search & browse with AI summaries
  • Annotate and review transparently
  • Organize collaborative workspaces
  • Explore funding opportunities
